ADVENT CALENDAR

This project is an electronic Advent Calendar designed to bring a little magic to your daily routine. Each day, you can open a new door to discover a special surprise, whether it’s warm wishes, motivational messages, or fun treats. Built with care using JavaScript, this calendar combines festive tradition with modern technology to make the countdown to your favorite occasion more exciting.

DEEP FOCUS

Deep Focus is a sleek and distraction-free timer designed to help you stay productive using the Pomodoro Technique. Its clean, minimalist interface keeps your focus on what matters most, while inspiring quotes motivate you during breaks and work sessions. Whether you’re tackling tasks, studying, or working on creative projects, Deep Focus helps you manage your time effectively and stay in the zone. Simplify your workflow and achieve more with ease!

SCHOOL DAY

This website was created as a “memory card” of a wonderful Self-Government Day. It serves as a reminder of the positive and memorable experiences of that day. The site reflects the atmosphere, the events, and the achievements of the self-government day, capturing the spirit of teamwork, leadership, and fun. It’s a place to look back on those moments and relive the best parts of this special day.

ECO FRIEND

EcoFriend is a web service that teaches the user how to properly sort waste. He motivates you to do this regularly with the help of a system of points, hints and game elements. The app uses AI to identify the type of garbage and track the user's progress. Based on the collected data, the service generates visual analytics and helps improve environmental habits.
